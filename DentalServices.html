DENTAL SERVICES

We Offer Services to Support the Needs of Your Teeth!

Dental implants are the perfect solution for you, if you are missing any teeth. They help encourage new bone growth, and are very easy to care for, and look natural. If you are a Richmond Hill, ON resident or a resident in the areas around Richmond Hill, you could consider Northern Dental to be the place to go for your dental implants, because we have dentists in our team that specialize in implants. Our team have been treating many dental implants for approximately 10 years, and we are looking forward to helping you too. If you are ready to fill up the gaps between your teeth, click or call to schedule your appointment at Northern Dental.

Invisalign is an advanced clear aligner orthodontic system. Invisalign uses a series of 3D printed clear aligners, to correct your smile and bite just like the metal braces. The only difference is metal braces are painful, and inconvenient. Invisalign, however is comfortable, unnoticeable, and suits your everyday lifestyle. Invisalign does not hurt, because Northern Dental uses the latest in digital scanning and sterolithography technology to apply only small forces to each tooth, which results in you feeling comfortable during your treatment. The Invisalign treatment timelines vary based upon the needs and wants of the individual and could range between 3-24 months. With Invisalign, you also don't have to change your diet at all, and eat whatever your heart desires.

At Northern Dental, we believe that regular dental cleanings can keep dental diseases from reaching your teeth. Your dental cleaning will include the removal of Calculus, which is hardened plaque that is left on the surface of the tooth for a period of time and is now strongly attached to the tooth's structure. At Northern Dental, we remove the calculus from your teeth, while stil making sure you have a healthy smile. We also remove plaque, which is a sticky film that forms on the teeth, which is filled with living bacteria, which produces toxins that could inflame the gums. Finally, we polish your teeth, which removes the surface stains and plaque on your teeth which is not removed when you brush your teeth.

Dental braces are the most common fixed appliance, which involves wires, bands and brackets. The bands are anchors that are held in place around your teeth and the brackets are attached to the front of your teeth. Both the brackets and the bands are connected to each other through archwires that the orthodontist tightens to put pressure on your teeth. This allows the teeth to slowly be aligned to the target position. Having dental braces requires you to regularly visit our office to tighten and adjust your braces. So if your teeth look out of place, don't hesitate to book an appointment with us, so that our team could give you the braces that will make your smile better!
